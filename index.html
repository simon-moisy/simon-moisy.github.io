05 Method

Most engagements start with an incomplete brief - a creative direction without a defined technical path. The first step is usually figuring out what the system actually needs to be: hardware constraints, latency budgets, failure handling, data flow between components. Architecture decisions made early tend to be the ones hardest to reverse later.

Work is typically contract-based and embedded within a studio or agency team. Scope ranges from full-stack ownership - backend, real-time runtime, computer vision, deployment - to focused engagements on a specific layer. Most projects involve some combination of Unity3D, software engineering, and a computer-vision or AI pipeline.

Available for extended on-site periods worldwide, with remote collaboration between visits. Open to projects in themed entertainment, brand experience, AI interaction, and architectural visualisation.
